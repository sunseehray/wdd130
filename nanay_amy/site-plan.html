Overview

Purpose

Nanay Amy has been doing housekeeping for a long time. From big families with children to young professionals, she has served a wide variety of clientele. Despite her expertise, not many people know about her and the specific services she offers. Hence, this website was developed to help build awareness on what Nanay Amy can do, how much a client is expected to pay, and how to contact her to avail of her services or to inquire about special requests. The website also aims to showcase the reviews that Nanay Amy has from her previous clients, so that new clients will have the confidence to avail of her services. Her services would include general cleaning, ironing and laundry, and cooking. There will also be a section that will showcase some of the unique services she offers such as gardening, catering, and disposal of old and bulky items.

Audience

Life in Bonifacio Global City can be very busy. With a large industry of IT professionals and BPO employees, the target audience for Nanay Amy's Housekeeping are families or young professionals who lack the time to do housekeeping. These potential clients also live within a 10-km radius. Since Nanay Amy is not a full-time stay-at-home service, her target audience also includes those who are only looking for part-time or stay-out housekeeper. As a one-woman service, potential clients also include those who want to have their hired housekeepers trained. Her service packages range from single-dwellers to a full household. She can also accept special requests from these same clients to serve a wider audience such as cooking for a large party. In case she is fully booked, clients can get waitlisted, and Nanay Amy will serve them on a first-come first-served basis depending on urgency as well.

Imagine finally being able to read that book you’ve been meaning to, catching up with old friends, and playing with those little ones who have been craving for attention. But a house must still be kept in order. Else, the stress won’t subside, things just pile up, and the cycle of frustration continues.

Fret no more. With Nanay Amy’s Housekeeping Services, you can buy yourself time - time for your physical and mental health, your loved ones, and even time for leisure. Because what’s the point of having all that money, if you’re just going to use it for stress-induced medical expenses? With Nanay Amy, you can have your house in order. You can finally demolish that mountain of laundry waiting to be folded. You can even plan parties and have Nanay Amy take care of the food, from grocery shopping to setting the scene.

With 30+ years of experience, Nanay Amy has honed the skills of keeping a house in good order. She values the quality of her work, so you know that your stuff is well taken care of. She has a cheerful personality that could enliven a home. She cooks well enough for a catering service.

Still in doubt? Don’t take our word for it. Just read what her past clients has to say about her work.

This website was built to help promote Nanay Amy’s housekeeping prowess and bless the lives of others who may need her services.

Services

Nanay Amy’s basic packages include the following:

Day Cleaning for 10sqm to 40sqm of space (500), for 41 to 80 sqm of space (1000), for 80 to 100 sqm of space (1250), for more than 100 sqm of space, please contact her. This includes cleaning supplies and use of equipment.

For laundry, her services cost 300 per 6kg. 150 per piece of duvet and other large items. Give her 48-72 hours to deliver your clothes depending on the weather conditions. Ironing costs 800 for a full day. 500 for a half day.

For cooking meals, she charges 10% of the groceries’ value. She charges a fee of 3000 per event of 150 pax including grocery shopping, delivery, cooking, and presentation.

If you’re in need of ready-to-eat meals or snacks, Nanay Amy can also cook her famous Laing, Sinaing na Tulingan, Kare-Kare, Gisadong Bagoong, Kinilaw na Langka, and Pork Binagoongan, Pork Menudo, Maja Blanca, Pancit, Biko, and Suman.

Contact Us

So, are you ready to take on Nanay Amy as your housekeeping arm? Booking her is easy, just contact her through this number and she will set up a schedule to visit or call you. Leave your name, address, contact number, service required and preferred time for consultation.

Don’t worry if you live outside of Makati or BGC, Nanay Amy’s Services is also available for those in Pasig and Mandaluyong. If you’re still outside of her coverage area, you may contact us, and we can make special arrangements.

With Nanay Amy, we offer money-back guarantee. If, for any reason, you were not satisfied with her work, contact us, and we’ll set you up for a refund.

In need of other services? Contact Nanay Amy and we can schedule an interview to get to know more about your concerns.
