Just this week, I was taking up business classes for my associate's in applied business management. Then, I decided to drop them and register for tech classes. I plan to pursue a bachelor's degree in applied technology instead.

I love BYUI because of the spirit. The advantage I see from a career in web design is that I can work from home. Plus, it's like solving puzzles, which I really enjoy despite it being frustrating at times.
